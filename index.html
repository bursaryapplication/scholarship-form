Your data is processed under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

The personal and sensitive information you provide (including orphan status, household income and disability) is collected solely to assess and administer this scholarship. It will be accessed only by the Selection Panel and authorised Kisii National Polytechnic officers bound by the Oath of Secrecy, retained for the period required by policy, and then securely disposed of. You may request access to or correction of your data through the Dean of Students.
